# U&Me Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2024

## Introduction

Welcome to U&Me ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use the U&Me mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
When you use U&Me, you create and store:
- Calendar events (titles, dates, times, descriptions)
- Event sharing preferences
- User preferences and settings
- Color choices and customizations

### Automatically Collected Information
We may collect:
- Device information (device type, operating system version)
- App usage statistics (features used, session duration)
- Crash reports and error logs
- Performance data

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not require account creation
- We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
- We do not collect location data
- We do not access your device contacts
- We do not access your device calendar (beyond what you create in-app)

## How We Store Your Information

### Local Storage
- All calendar events and user data are stored locally on your device
- Data is encrypted at rest using your device's security features
- We do not store your personal data on our servers

### No Cloud Sync (Current Version)
- Data is not automatically backed up to cloud services
- Events are not synchronized across devices
- If you delete the app, all data is removed

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide the calendar sharing functionality
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Analyze app usage to develop new features
- Provide customer support

## Information Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Provide your information to data brokers
- Use your data for marketing purposes

We MAY share information with:
- **Service Providers**: Analytics services (Expo, Google Analytics) that help us improve the app
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law, court order, or legal process
- **Safety**: To protect rights, property, or safety of our users or others

## Third-Party Services

U&Me is built using:
- **Expo** - Development framework ([Privacy Policy](https://expo.dev/privacy))
- **React Native** - Mobile framework ([Privacy Policy](https://reactnative.dev/privacy))

These services may collect anonymous usage data. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored in the app
- **Delete**: Remove all data via the Profile screen or by uninstalling the app
- **Control**: Choose what events to create and share
- **Opt-Out**: Disable analytics in app settings (if available)

### How to Delete Your Data
1. Open U&Me app
2. Go to Profile screen
3. Tap "Clear All Data"
   
OR

Simply uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data.

## Children's Privacy

U&Me does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app does not require personal information to function. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us immediately.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:
- Local data encryption using device security
- Secure communication protocols (HTTPS)
- Regular security updates
- No server-side data storage (reducing risk)

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## International Data Transfers

Since data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. Any analytics data collected by third-party services (Expo, etc.) may be transferred internationally according to their privacy policies.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on our website. The "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly.

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated privacy policy.

## Your Consent

By using U&Me, you consent to this privacy policy.
